INSTRUCTIONS TO APPLY ONLINE

1.    This online application is applicable only for students who registered for the current semester or applying for suppli/betterment examinations.
2. Only online applications submitted by completing all 4-STEPS in application form and paid the appropriate fee are considered.
3. You have to apply separately for Regular,Supplementary, and Betterment examinations. Fee has to be paid separately.You will get separate application Ref.no for each.
4. After successful submission of online application in STEP 4, please note down the application reference number displayed on the top of the form, this number will be the future corresponding number for any quarries regarding the examination you are applying.
5. Students who are having backlogs only can apply for supplementary exams. Students who detained the course or studying the course now, are not eligible under supplementary exams. They need to apply under “Regular” scheme only. In case your course is not updated or not displaying , please do contact the office of Controller of Exams.
6. For quarries regarding the online application process, drop a mail to coeoffice@kluniversity.in, mentioning your university roll number ,name and branch. If you have a quarry about the submitted application form,mention your online application reference number and SBH collect reference number along with roll number and name.
